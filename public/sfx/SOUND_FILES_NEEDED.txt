Sound Effects Needed for Megapede Game:

1. bullet-shoot.wav - Regular bullet firing sound
2. plasma-shoot.wav - Upgraded/plasma bullet firing sound  
3. enemy-hit.wav - Sound when bullet hits enemy body/segment
4. armor-hit.wav - Sound when bullet hits armored enemy (metallic clang)
5. block-destroy.wav - Sound when mushroom/obstacle block is destroyed
6. player-death.wav - Sound when player dies/crashes (optional)
7. power-up.wav - Sound when picking up power-ups (optional)
8. energy-activate.wav - Sound when player becomes energized (dramatic power-up)
9. energy-deactivate.wav - Sound when energy mode expires (power-down)

Please add these sound files to the /public/sfx/ folder.
Supported formats: .wav, .mp3, .ogg
